Frog Quest

Frog Quest is the working name for an adventure game by Tri Dragon Programming.

Built after the style of games like The Legend of Zelda: The Minish Cap, this full-length game follows the adventure of a young frog who must save his people from an evil snake. This game, which can be played as either single player or with a partner, is sure to lead to memories.

Estimated to be released in late 2026 or early 2027.
